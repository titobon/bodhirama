Biography of Ajahn Visalo

Ajahn Visalo is an Indonesian monk from Wat Pah Nanachat. Born in 1977 in Central Java, he came to know and practice meditation from several traditions from the age of 20. At 29, he encountered Buddhism through Ajahn Chah's Dhamma Book and ordained as a Samanera in 2006. He was granted full ordination by Luang Por Liem, the Abbot of Wat Nong Pah Pong, Thailand in 2008.
